About our butchershop

Since our opening in 2016, our butchershop offers fresh Zabiha Halal meats: Goat, Beef, Lamb, & Chicken. Our butchershop is open from 9:00 am to 6:00 pm but we prepare trays of meat for customers coming in after 6:00 pm. If you would like to place an order call (817) 508-8861 anytime before 6:00 pm.
