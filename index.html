Who we are

Nadine Halal Market is a Mediterranean grocery store that specializes in Zabiha Halal meats such as beef, lamb, chicken and goat. We carry products from all over the Middle East, North Africa, Southeast Asia, India and Pakistan.

About Us

Nadine Halal Market is a family owned and family run grocery store serving the mid cities Muslim and general community since 2016. Our offering of freshly cut Zabiha Halal meats, fresh produce, as well as exclusive Mediterranean items allowed us to build a loyal customer base. In August of 2021 we opened up our new store and we are looking forward to continuing serving the community in our new location. Please don't hesitate to contact us if you have any questions.
